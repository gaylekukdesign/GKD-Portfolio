The Vine
Web Design

The Vine helps students prepare for higher education by guiding them to make the right choice for their college plan. The core value of its services is assisting young students in finding their genuine passion and developing their potential for positive changes and personal growth despite the fact that they are in the midst of the competitive college admissions environment.

This project was to redesign the old Vine website to improve the overall look of visuals, content arrangement, and user experience. To reflect positiveness in the business and to engage with young students, the illustrations, icons and colors are welcoming, pleasant, and cheerful.

The Vine
Web Design
The Vine began to help students prepare for higher education and guide them to make a right choice for their college plan. The core value of its services is assisting young students in finding thier genuine passion and develop their potentials for positive change and growth in life despite the fact that they are in the middle of the competitive and over-heated college admissions environment.

This project aimed to redesign the old vine website and improve the overall look of visuals, content arrangement, and user experience. To reflect the positive & earnest attributes of the business and engage with young students, the illustrations, vine icons and colors are welcoming, pleasant, and uplifting.

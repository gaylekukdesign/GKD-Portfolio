Robotology
Motion Graphics

This video was created to promote a fictional history book based on real events, Robotology. This book is about the first humanoid robot, 'the robotic knight' by Da Vinci up to the most advanced humanoid, Asimo by Honda.

I focused on the aspects of time and space where Da Vinci's knight and Asimo connected through dream and fantasy as human beings beyond time and space.

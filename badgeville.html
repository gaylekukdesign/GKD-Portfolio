Badgeville Connector for Yammer
Web|UX Design

Badgeville for Yammer extends the social collaboration platform for a more dynamic experience by rewarding participation to help the company achieve important business objectives. Setting up Badgeville Connector is the first step in connecting the Badgeville experience to the Yammer environment.

Our team was challenged to come up with a solution to guide the administration of an easy and simple deployment of the app because many administrative steps and points need to be followed. My role was designing the landing page and connector UX/UI.

Yammer is an enterprise social networking service used for collaboration and communication within organizations. It allows companies to bring teams together, collaborate on files, and organize projects fast and efficiently.

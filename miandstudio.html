MiAND Studio
Web Design

MiAND Studio is an art and design education center that helps young students prepare for higher education in art and design fields through well-developed curriculums and in-depth training.

This website is built to connect with students and parents and provide information and updates from the studio. The visual focus was to use bold and solid color swatches & design to create a look that portrays 'trust' as an educational service provider. Despite that, its design illustrates fun and creative aspects of the center an art and design studio.

MiAND Studio
Web Design

MiAND Studio is an art and design education center that helps young students to prepare for advanced study in art and design fields through a well-developed curriculum and with hands-on training.

This website is built to connect with students and parents and to provide information and updates about the studio. The visual focus was to create a look that inspired 'trust' through bold and solid color swatches, reflecting the fun and creative aspects appropriate of an art and design studio.

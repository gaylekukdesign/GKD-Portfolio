SF Japantown
Motion Graphics

This is a promotional video for Japantown SF through a typographic approach. I wrote a short poem about Japantown SF, and put it in a mobile form combining with icons of Japanese family crests. I was inspired by what Alexander Calder said: "To most people who look at a mobile, it's no more than a series of flat objects that move. To a few, though, it may be poetry."

SF Japantown
Motion Graphics

This project is a promotional video for Japantown SF using a typographic approach. Inspired by what Alexander Calder said: "To most people who look at a mobile, it's no more than a series of flat objects that move. To a few, though, it may be poetry", I wrote a short poem about Japantown SF and put it in a mobile form combining it with icons of Japanese family crests.

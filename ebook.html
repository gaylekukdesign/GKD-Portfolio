Gamification Workbook
eBook

This workbook is created by Badgeville to provide a better understanding of gamification, and to help a company to set a goal for its online community.It illustrates the power of online communities, and how gamification can boost engagement and loyalty to brands and businesses. Two main contents, gamification information and questions to set a goal, guide companies to understand what gamification is and what needs to be considered leverage and engage the thriving online community for the company.

The visual theme of a "Road Trip" invites companies to join us on the journey of creating a gamification system and enjoying the time together build its prosperous online community.

Role
Visual Designer
Goals to Acheive
    Design an e-book promoting gamification as a powerful tool for an online comminity
    Use engaging design that helps readers through the workbook in a fun and easy way
    Create a visual theme that doesn't have 'computer game' visuals often associated with gamification

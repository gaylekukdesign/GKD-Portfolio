I have been attending New Community Baptist Church past 13 years and it is the most important community that makes my life meaningful and purposeful.
This Project started as a form of serving using my skills of design and coding. started with the people who use this product and service. Our users were encouraged to upload their art and design work so our focus was to design a simple and intuitive uploading experience. The other important aspect of using the website is the ability to search portfolios by the school and major of admission. Since each page contains an ample collection of thumbnail images and portfolio information, I opted for a clean and organized layout design.

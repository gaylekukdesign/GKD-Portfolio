Gamification Workbook
eBook

This workbook was created by Badgeville to help companies set smart goals for their online community by providing the user with a better understanding of gamification. It introduces the power of online communities in the technological age and how gamification can exponentially boost engagement and loyalty to brands and businesses. There are two main sections that were illustrated; first, an introduction to gamification and second, goal-setting questions that should be considered for a healthy, thriving online community.

I chose the visual theme as "Road Trip" to suggest companies join us in the journey of creating a gamification system where you can enjoy the adventure of building a prosperous online community.
